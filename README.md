## 📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes a **Portuguese banking dataset** to uncover insights about:

- Customer financial behavior  
- Loan impact on life quality  
- Education-level differences  
- Marketing campaign effectiveness  

The dataset contains **41,188 records and 20 variables** :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Banks need to understand:
- Which customers are more financially stable  
- How loans affect customer life quality  
- Which segments are more likely to invest  

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- 🎓 **Tertiary education group shows highest financial stability**
- 💰 Most customers have balance **below 40,000**
- ⚠️ Loans and defaults significantly impact life quality  
- 📊 Education level influences financial behavior
